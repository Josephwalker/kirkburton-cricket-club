Membership subscriptions

To become a member of Kirkburton Cricket Club please choose the type of membership required. Once you have selected the membership type you will be ask to fill in your details or the details of your child. After you have filled in your details you will be directed to Paypal to pay for your membership, here you can pay by Paypal, credit or debit card.
